Starcube

An ambisonic experience in form of a spatial game

Through a cube in the middle of a room with 8 speakers around, the player controls the game that just happens sonicially. An energy-ball enters the room and the player activates invisible walls to catch the ball and let it bounce inside the room as long as possible.
